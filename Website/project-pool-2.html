Project Pool Day 2

This project covers the fundamentals of Communcation & Presentation, Interactive Development and Design and Visualisation.


For this task we chose to do a drum machine. The sounds are placed and sourced inside < audio > elements in the HTML file. In the javascript we added querySelectors to the sound elements and used a function called play(); to play them once a certain key is pressed. In this case the A, S and D keys are used and play the sounds allocated to them once a key press is detected on each element.

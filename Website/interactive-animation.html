Interactive Animation

This project covers the fundamentals of Communcation & Presentation, Interactive Development and Design and Visualisation.


Alex Lenta and & created this interactive animation that consists of chained events. The animation is coded with event-listeners, play(); functions and arrays. The basis of the animaton happens in an animation frame < div >, where spritesheets and images are placed on with different z-indexes to make the scene come to life. The story has a linear progression where it continues animation once the previous animation has finished.
